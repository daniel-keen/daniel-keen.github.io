Block Line Up

Challenge your brain with Block Line Up, the addictive puzzle game that will keep you coming back for more! With a grid of 9 by 9 squares and a variety of block shapes to choose from, you'll need to think strategically to fill up lines either vertically or horizontally. The fast-paced gameplay and simple controls make it easy to get started, but hard to master. With endless levels and increasing difficulty, Block Line Up will keep you entertained for hours.
